# 📊 HR Analytics - Employee Attrition Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze employee attrition patterns, workforce demographics, and organizational trends. This project provides data-driven insights to help organizations understand employee turnover and improve retention strategies.

---

## 🚀 Project Overview

Employee attrition significantly impacts organizational productivity and business performance. This dashboard analyzes HR data to identify the key factors influencing employee turnover and provides actionable recommendations for improving employee retention.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze employee attrition across departments, job roles, and salary groups.
* Identify workforce trends and high-risk employee segments.
* Explore the relationship between employee demographics and attrition.
* Generate business insights to support data-driven HR decision-making.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* **Microsoft Power BI**
* **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)**
* **Microsoft Excel**

---

## 📌 Dashboard Features

✔️ KPI Cards (Total Employees, Attrition Count, Attrition Rate, Average Age, Average Salary, Average Years at Company)

✔️ Department-wise Attrition Analysis

✔️ Education Field Analysis

✔️ Salary Slab Analysis

✔️ Job Level Analysis

✔️ Attrition by Years at Company

✔️ Interactive Filters and Slicers

✔️ Business Insights & Recommendations

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* Research & Development department recorded the highest employee attrition.
* Employees earning below **5K salary** exhibited the highest turnover rate.
* Job Level 1 employees experienced maximum attrition.
* Early-career employees showed a higher tendency to leave the organization.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Introduce targeted retention programs for high-attrition employee groups.
* Review compensation strategies for lower salary bands.
* Strengthen employee engagement initiatives for early-career professionals.
* Conduct regular employee feedback and satisfaction surveys.
